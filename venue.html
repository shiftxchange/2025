Middlesex University Mauritius, located in the vibrant and picturesque tourist destination of Flic en Flac, in collaboration with STADIO Higher Education and Business Mauritius, is delighted to invite you to the International Conference ShiftXchange 2025: Leading with Purpose: Sustainable Practices for a Resilient Future. Nestled along the west coast of Mauritius, Flic en Flac boasts one of the island's longest beaches, offering an exceptional backdrop for our gathering. This serene setting is perfect for attendees to not only engage in meaningful discussions but also to enjoy activities such as diving or snorkeling in the stunning blue lagoon and coral reef surrounding the area.
