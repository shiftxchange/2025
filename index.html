ShiftXchange 2025

Leading with Purpose: Sustainable Practices for a Resilient Future

1 - 3 September 2025, Middlesex University Mauritius

ShiftXchange 2025 International conference places a sharp focus on the intersection of leadership and sustainability, recognizing the critical need for leaders who can navigate and integrate ethical, economic, and environmental dimensions into their decision-making processes. The event aims to provide leaders with the knowledge and tools to implement practices that support long-term economic growth, environmental stewardship, and social equity, aligning closely with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Through a series of keynote speeches, panel discussions, and collaborative workshops, participants will examine current challenges and opportunities in sustainable leadership. The conference will highlight innovative strategies that leaders can adopt to ensure their organizations not only thrive but also positively impact their communities and the environment. ShiftXchange 2025 is more than just a conference—it's a commitment to cultivating leadership that is visionary, responsible, and deeply engaged with the most pressing issues of our time.

Sub-themes of the conference include:

  • Leadership strategies for financial stability and long-term economic growth
  • Ethical leadership in creating policies and laws supporting sustainable practices
  • Leadership to align industries and institutions with the UN’s SDGs
  • Leadership in aligning organisational goals with long-term visions
  • Balancing organisational objectives with employee well-being
  • Leadership inspiring a shift toward sustainable mindsets across industries
  • Leadership to integrate cutting-edge technologies for sustainable development
  • Leadership and the evolving demands of purpose-driven organisations
  • Leadership for sustainable practices and reform in academic institutions
  • Leadership practices in HE fostering partnerships in community-based projects

ShiftXchange 2025 Features:

  • Conference presentations during 3 full days
  • Events for speed dating and networking, as well as conference dinner and excursion
