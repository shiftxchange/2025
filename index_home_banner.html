27 – 30 ______ 2025, Middlesex University Mauritius

ShiftXchange 2025 International conference aims to create a dynamic platform for academics, practitioners, and industry leaders to engage in thought-provoking discussions and collaborative exploration of innovative strategies, practices, and technologies shaping leadership in the digital age. This conference will promote interdisciplinary dialogue, sharing of insights, and forging of partnerships to address the evolving challenges and opportunities in leadership, with a focus on practical applications and real-world implications in the digital landscape. It will cover various dimensions of leadership, including organizational leadership, digital transformation, technological advancements, and societal impact, aiming to inspire innovative approaches and actionable insights for navigating the complexities of leadership in today's rapidly evolving digital environment.

Sub-themes of the conference include:

Sub-theme one: Resilient enterprises – leading for long-term success
  • Sustainable business leadership: Integrating ethical and forward-thinking strategies into commercial success
  • Purpose-driven innovation: Aligning research and business for growth with lasting impact
  • Building strategic partnerships: Collaborative leadership for business resilience and market adaptation
Sub-theme two: Expanding the frontiers of progress
  • Innovative leadership for the future: Driving ethical and responsible practices across industries by avoiding depletion of natural, financial or human resources
  • Restoring balance: Leadership approaches to address global challenges, from climate change to economic stability, resource efficiency and conservation
  • Circular thinking and beyond: Integrating smart resource management into business and societal strategies
Sub-theme three: Governance for global progress
  • Policy leadership for impact: Crafting regulations that drive economic, social, and environmental resilience
  • International collaboration for a thriving future: Strategies for effective global and cross-sector partnerships
  • Adaptive governance: Navigating policy challenges for innovation, growth, and long-term stability

ShiftXchange 2025 Features:

  • 2 International post-conference workshops
  • Conference presentations during 3 full days
  • 5 Plenary sessions, including 2 keynote addresses
  • Events for speed dating and networking, as well as conference dinner and excursion
