Abstracts must be written in English and should be a maximum of 500 words, followed by a list of maximum 5 keywords (separated by semi-colons). Submitted abstracts should include the title of the research, details of authors (Full name, Affiliation, Email Address) and should be followed by an overview of the research, structured in different paragraphs including the purpose of the research, methodology, key findings and recommendations. For referencing, APA7 style should be used.

Formatting guidelines are as follows:

  • Title: Times New Roman, size 16 font, bold and centred at the top of the page.
  • Author Names: Centered, listed with first name, middle initial, and surname. Multiple authors separated by commas.
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12-point size, single line spacing.
  • Page Layout: A4 size (297 Ã— 210mm).
All abstracts should be submitted on the submission link (click the button below to make your submission)
All abstracts will be reviewed by the Scientific Committee and the corresponding author will be notified of acceptance.
