The ShiftXchange Conference, jointly offered by STADIO Higher Education and Middlesex University, is scheduled to take place at Middlesex University in Mauritius on 1, 2, and 3 September 2025. Building on the success of the 2024 conference, it aims to attract a diverse range of presenters and participants from around the globe, along with an engaging audience. This year, the conference will once again feature thought-provoking workshops. In addition to parallel sessions for full research papers, it will also include poster presentations and open mic sessions. During these open mic sessions, presenters can introduce their research projects or works-in-progress, fostering scholarly conversations in a “round table” format that encourages idea generation and allows for diverse perspectives.
All submissions will undergo a peer review process led by experts in the Scientific Committee. Authors will be notified of paper acceptance via email following the review process.

Sub-themes of the conference include:

Sub-theme one: Resilient enterprises – leading for long-term success
  • Sustainable business leadership: Integrating ethical and forward-thinking strategies into commercial success
  • Purpose-driven innovation: Aligning research and business for growth with lasting impact
  • Building strategic partnerships: Collaborative leadership for business resilience and market adaptation
Sub-theme two: Expanding the frontiers of progress
  • Innovative leadership for the future: Driving ethical and responsible practices across industries by avoiding depletion of natural, financial or human resources
  • Restoring balance: Leadership approaches to address global challenges, from climate change to economic stability, resource efficiency and conservation
  • Circular thinking and beyond: Integrating smart resource management into business and societal strategies
Sub-theme three: Governance for global progress
  • Policy leadership for impact: Crafting regulations that drive economic, social, and environmental resilience
  • International collaboration for a thriving future: Strategies for effective global and cross-sector partnerships
  • Adaptive governance: Navigating policy challenges for innovation, growth, and long-term stability
ShiftXchange 2025 Features:
  • Conference presentations during 3 full days
  • Participating in Workshops: Engage in interactive workshops that discuss relevant topics.
  • Joining Open Mic Sessions: Participate in dynamic open mic sessions where research and projects are debated.
  • Exploring Poster Presentations: View and discuss research findings and innovative projects through poster presentations.
  • Events for speed dating and networking, including conference dinner and excursion
