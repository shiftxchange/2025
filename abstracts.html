STADIO Higher Education, based in South Africa, in collaboration with Middlesex University Mauritius, is proud to announce the International Conference, ShiftXchange 2025: A Practice-Based Approach to Rethinking Leadership in the Digital Era. The conference is focused on the theme of collaborative solutions in the evolving digital landscape and aims to promote knowledge sharing, highlight challenges, and showcase best practices. The Call for Abstracts is currently open and we welcome contributions from academics, educators, researchers, business professionals, practitioners, innovators, leaders, and organizations with valuable insights and experiences to share.
All submissions will undergo a peer review process led by experts in the Scientific Committee. Authors will be notified of paper acceptance via email following the review process.

Download Call for Abstracts
Sub-themes of the conference include:
  • Leadership strategies for financial stability and long-term economic growth
  • Ethical leadership in creating policies and laws supporting sustainable practices
  • Leadership to align industries and institutions with the UN’s SDGs
  • Leadership in aligning organisational goals with long-term visions
  • Balancing organisational objectives with employee well-being
  • Leadership inspiring a shift toward sustainable mindsets across industries
  • Leadership to integrate cutting-edge technologies for sustainable development
  • Leadership and the evolving demands of purpose-driven organisations
  • Leadership for sustainable practices and reform in academic institutions
  • Leadership practices in HE fostering partnerships in community-based projects
ShiftXchange 2025 Features:
  • Conference presentations during 3 full days
  • Events for speed dating and networking, including conference dinner and excursion
